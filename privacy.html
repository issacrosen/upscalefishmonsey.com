Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 7/03/2025

Information We Collect

Upscale Fish ("we," "us," or "our") collects information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Create an account or place an order
  • Sign up for our SMS messaging program
  • Contact us for customer service
  • Visit our store location

Information collected may include:

  • Name and contact information (phone number, email address, mailing address)
  • Order history and preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party processors)
  • SMS opt-in preferences and messaging history

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send you SMS notifications about order status, special offers, and store updates
  • Provide customer service and support
  • Improve our products and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

SMS Messaging Program

When you opt into our SMS messaging program, you consent to receive text messages from Upscale Fish including:

  • Order confirmations and updates
  • Weekly special offers and promotions
  • Store announcements and notifications

Message frequency: Approximately 1 message per week, with additional order-related messages as needed.

SMS Data Sharing: Mobile information will not be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

How to Opt Out

If you wish to be removed from receiving future SMS communications, you can opt out by:

  • Texting STOP, QUIT, END, REVOKE, OPT OUT, CANCEL, or UNSUBSCRIBE to any message
  • Calling us at (845) 352-2555
  • Emailing us at orders@upscalefishmarket.com
  • Visiting our store at 126 Maple Ave #7, Spring Valley, NY 10977

Information Security

We implement reasonable physical, administrative, and technical security controls to protect and secure your personal information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure.
